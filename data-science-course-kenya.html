Why Learn Data Science?

Data science is one of the fastest-growing careers globally. The U.S. Bureau of Labor Statistics projects 34% job growth for data scientists, far above average across professions.

Organizations across banking, telecom, startups, and government increasingly rely on data to make decisions — creating strong demand for people who can analyze and interpret data.

In Kenya, data science roles are among the highest-paying entry-level tech careers, with salaries often starting above KES 80,000 depending on skills and experience.

What You’ll Learn

This course focuses on practical data science skills used in industry. You’ll learn how to:

  • Analyze real datasets
  • Write Python for data analysis
  • Query databases using SQL
  • Visualize insights
  • Build machine learning models
  • Present results clearly

By the end of the program, you will have a portfolio of real projects.
